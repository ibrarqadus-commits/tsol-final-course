Complaints Procedure

Last updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

The Streets of London is committed to providing high-quality educational and mentoring services. However, we recognise that there may be occasions when a student or client feels dissatisfied with the service received. This Complaints Procedure explains how we will handle such matters fairly, promptly, and professionally.

2. Our Commitment

We aim to:

  • Handle all complaints in a transparent and courteous manner.
  • Investigate complaints promptly and objectively.
  • Provide a clear written response with proposed resolutions.

3. Making a Complaint

If you wish to make a complaint, please submit it in writing to:

The Streets of London
Email: thestreetsoflondon112@gmail.com
Subject line: "Formal Complaint"

Please include the following details:

  • Your full name and contact details.
  • A clear description of your complaint.
  • Relevant dates, screenshots, or documents (if applicable).
  • Your desired outcome or resolution.

4. Acknowledgement

We will acknowledge receipt of your complaint within 5 working days and aim to provide a full written response within 15 working days of acknowledgment.

5. Investigation Process

Your complaint will be investigated by the principal of The Streets of London or an appointed senior representative. We may contact you for additional information if necessary. A final decision will be communicated in writing.

6. Escalation

If you remain dissatisfied with the outcome, you may request an independent review. The Streets of London is a professional training provider and aims to resolve disputes internally; however, if regulatory membership is applicable (e.g. The Property Ombudsman or other redress body), details will be provided upon request.

7. Confidentiality

All complaints are handled in strict confidence and in accordance with our Privacy Policy and data protection obligations under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

8. Continuous Improvement

Feedback and complaints are used to improve the quality of our training, mentoring, and online services. We are committed to ensuring a fair and transparent learning environment for all students and clients.

9. Contact

For all complaints or follow-up communications, please contact:

The Streets of London
Email: thestreetsoflondon112@gmail.com
Jurisdiction: England and Wales
