Accessibility Statement

Last updated: October 2025

1. Commitment to Accessibility

The Streets of London ("we", "us", "our") is committed to ensuring that our website, www.thestreetsoflondon.co.uk ("the Site"), is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive online learning environment in line with the Equality Act 2010 and current best practices for digital accessibility.

2. Accessibility Standards

We aim to comply with internationally recognised accessibility standards, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at level AA, where possible.

3. Measures to Support Accessibility

To make our Site accessible, we have implemented the following measures:

  • Text alternatives for non-text content where applicable.
  • Clear navigation and consistent layout throughout the website.
  • High-contrast design options for readability.
  • Keyboard navigable interfaces and accessible forms.
  • Regular testing of accessibility features across major browsers and devices.

4. Limitations and Exceptions

While we strive to make our website fully accessible, some content or third-party tools may not yet fully meet accessibility standards. We are continuously reviewing and improving our platform to minimise barriers.

5. Feedback

If you experience difficulties accessing any part of the Site or our courses, please contact us with your feedback. We will work to resolve accessibility issues promptly.
